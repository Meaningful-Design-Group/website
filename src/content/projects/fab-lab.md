## About Fab Lab Bali

Founded in 2012, Fab Lab Bali is the first Fab Lab in Southeast Asia and one of the most active in the global [Fab Lab network](https://fabfoundation.org). We are a fully equipped digital fabrication laboratory offering laser cutting, 3D printing, CNC milling, electronics prototyping, and much more.

## What We Offer

### Maker Education
Year-round training programs from introductory workshops to the globally certified Fab Academy — a six-month intensive digital fabrication program taught at MIT and 150+ Fab Labs worldwide.

### Open Lab Days
Weekly open studio sessions for community members, students, and professionals to use our equipment, get support, and connect with fellow makers.

### Research & Development
Collaborative R&D with industry partners, universities, and NGOs on locally relevant design challenges — from biocomposite materials to affordable construction systems.

### Community Projects
Maker education initiatives with local schools, artisans, and community groups — developing skills and creating economic opportunities through design and fabrication.

## Equipment

Our lab is equipped with industry-standard digital fabrication tools:
- **Laser Cutters** — for precision cutting of wood, acrylic, fabric, and more
- **3D Printers** — FDM and resin printing for rapid prototyping
- **CNC Router** — large-format milling for furniture, architecture, and signage
- **Vinyl Cutters** — for stickers, heat transfer, and sign-making
- **Electronics Lab** — PCB fabrication, soldering, and microcontroller programming
- **Sewing & Textiles** — embroidery machines and material experiments

## Network

As an official Fab Lab, we're part of a global network of 2,000+ labs sharing knowledge, tools, and projects. We host researchers and makers from around the world and connect Balinese makers to international opportunities.
