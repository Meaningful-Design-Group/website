## What is a Learning Journey?

A Learning Journey is a short, intensive immersive program designed for teams, cohorts, and organisations who want to experience the intersection of design, making, and community in Bali.

Over 5 to 14 days, participants move between Fab Lab Bali, village communities, rice terraces, craft workshops, and cultural sites — building new ways of thinking and working through hands-on experience.

## Who Comes on Learning Journeys?

- **Corporate innovation teams** — exploring distributed manufacturing, circular economy, and design thinking in an immersive context
- **University cohorts** — field programs for design, architecture, and sustainability students
- **Social entrepreneurs** — exploring community-centred design and locally-led innovation
- **Executives & leaders** — leadership development through making and community engagement

## Sample Itinerary (10-day)

**Days 1–2:** Orientation, Fab Lab introduction, Balinese culture and Tri Hita Karana
**Days 3–4:** Digital fabrication intensive — laser cutting, 3D printing, electronics
**Days 5–6:** Village immersion — traditional craft systems, rice terrace ecology
**Days 7–8:** Design challenge with community partner (real problem, real stakes)
**Days 9–10:** Prototype, present, and celebrate

## What You'll Take Home

- A portfolio of made objects and documentation
- New frameworks for community-centred design
- Relationships with makers and innovators across Bali and the globe
- A renewed sense of what design can do in the world
