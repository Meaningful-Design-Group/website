## About the Programme

MDG's Vocational Training programme provides practical digital fabrication skills for young Balinese who want to build careers in the creative economy — from product design and manufacturing to digital entrepreneurship.

The programme is designed to be accessible: held in the evenings and on weekends, taught in Indonesian and Balinese, and with scholarships available for participants who cannot afford course fees.

## What You'll Learn

### Module 1: Digital Design Fundamentals
- Computer-aided design (CAD) basics
- 2D design with Inkscape and Illustrator
- Product design principles

### Module 2: Fabrication Skills
- Laser cutting and engraving
- 3D printing and rapid prototyping
- Vinyl cutting and applied graphics

### Module 3: Electronics & Making
- Introduction to electronics and microcontrollers
- Building functional products with sensors and actuators
- Basic programming with Arduino

### Module 4: Business & Entrepreneurship
- Product development and prototyping
- Pricing, costing, and business basics
- Online selling and marketing for makers
- Connecting to local and global markets

## Outcomes

Graduates receive:
- A vocational certificate in Digital Fabrication from MDG
- Portfolio of products made during the programme
- Access to Fab Lab Bali equipment for ongoing projects
- Connection to MDG's network of clients and collaborators

## Scholarship & Support

We offer full and partial scholarships for young Balinese participants. Priority given to women, those from economically marginalised backgrounds, and applicants with strong motivation to build maker-based livelihoods.
